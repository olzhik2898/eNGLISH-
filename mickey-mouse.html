Directions ‚ñº

Watch Mickey Mouse's magic show and learn new words about magic and surprises!

Learning Objectives:

  • Learn 6 new vocabulary words about magic
  • Practice present continuous tense
  • Discuss favorite magic tricks
  • Improve listening skills

Questions ‚ñº

1. What does Mickey pull out of his magic hat?

2. Who helps Mickey with the magic show?

3. What happens at the end of the show?

Grammar ‚ñº

Present Continuous Tense

We use Present Continuous to talk about actions happening now.

Complete the sentences:

Mickey magic tricks now.
Donald the show.
They for Mickey.

Discussion ‚ñº

Talk about these questions:

  • Do you like magic shows? Why or why not?
  • What is your favorite magic trick?
  • Have you ever tried to do a magic trick?
  • Who is your favorite character in Mickey Mouse?
