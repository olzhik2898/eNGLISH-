Grammar ‚ñº

Using "Can" for Ability

We use "can" to talk about what someone is able to do.

Complete the sentences:

Chase use his police skills.
Skye fly in her helicopter.
They work as a team.

Discussion ‚ñº

Talk about these questions:

  • Why is teamwork important in rescue missions?
  • Which Paw Patrol character is your favorite? Why?
  • Have you ever helped someone? What did you do?
  • What special skills do you have that can help others?
