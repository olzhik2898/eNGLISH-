Directions ‚ñº

Watch the video and complete the exercises below. Pay attention to the vocabulary words and how they are used in the story.

Learning Objectives:

  • Learn 6 new vocabulary words about picnics
  • Practice listening comprehension
  • Build sentences using new words
  • Discuss personal experiences

Questions ‚ñº

1. Where did Peppa and her family go for the picnic?

2. What did they bring in the basket?

3. Who found the ants?

Grammar ‚ñº

Present Simple Tense

We use Present Simple to talk about habits and routines.

Complete the sentences:

Peppa picnics.
They food in a basket.
George with his toys.

Discussion ‚ñº

Talk about these questions:

  • Do you like picnics? Why or why not?
  • What is your favorite food to bring on a picnic?
  • Where is the best place for a picnic in your city?
  • Have you ever had any funny experiences during a picnic?
