Directions ‚ñº

Join SpongeBob at the Krusty Krab and learn words about work and friendship underwater!

Learning Objectives:

  • Learn 6 new vocabulary words about work and food
  • Practice using adjectives to describe things
  • Discuss friendship and teamwork
  • Improve comprehension of humor and expressions

Questions ‚ñº

2. Who is SpongeBob's best friend?

Grammar ‚ñº

Adjectives for Description

We use adjectives to describe people, places, and things.

Complete the sentences with adjectives:

SpongeBob is very and happy.
The Krusty Krab is a restaurant.
Krabby Patties are and delicious.

Discussion ‚ñº

Talk about these questions:

  • What makes SpongeBob a good friend?
  • Would you like to work at the Krusty Krab? Why?
  • What's your favorite food from a restaurant?
  • How do you make people laugh like SpongeBob?
